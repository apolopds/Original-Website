Reflection

Reflection in Words

Hello everyone! I’m here to share my insights from our midterm lessons about the Internet and the World Wide Web.

I learned that the internet connects many smaller networks around the world and works through servers, clients, and IP addresses to send information.

I also learned about the difference between narrowband and broadband — narrowband is slow and outdated, while broadband is the fast internet we use today from providers like PLDT and Globe.

Computer Hardware Components Computer hardware includes all the physical parts like the CPU, memory, and storage. I learned how these parts work together to make the computer run properly.

Classification and Function of Hardware and Software Hardware performs actions while software gives instructions. Both are important because they depend on each other to work.

Integration of Hardware and Software Hardware acts as the body, and software is the brain that controls it. When they work together, the computer functions smoothly.

The World Wide Web is not the same as the internet — it’s just one part where we can browse, watch videos, and read pages through browsers.

There are many ways to communicate online like email, messaging, video calls, and social media, which make sharing information easier.

I also realized how powerful the internet is for business, learning, and entertainment — but it can be risky because of threats like phishing, spamming, and spyware.

Overall, I learned that the internet is very useful, but we must use it wisely and safely.

Thank you for listening!
